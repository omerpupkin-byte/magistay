Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who we are

Magistay ("we", "our", "us") operates the website magistay.com — a free AI-powered hotel matching service. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the information you provide during the hotel search quiz: destination, travel dates, number and ages of travelers, preferences, and any free-text notes you choose to enter. We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address.

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data or track individuals across devices.

3. How we use your data

Quiz data is sent to our server solely to generate hotel recommendations. We do not store your quiz answers after the session ends. We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Affiliate links

When you click a "Book on [platform]" button, you are redirected to that platform via an affiliate link. We may earn a commission if you complete a booking. This does not affect the price you pay. We have affiliate relationships with Booking.com, Expedia, Agoda, Hotels.com, TripAdvisor, and Kayak.

5. Third-party services

Our AI matching is powered by Anthropic's Claude API. Quiz data is transmitted to Anthropic's servers for processing and is subject to Anthropic's privacy policy. Hotel data is sourced from Booking.com and Expedia partner APIs.

6. Cookies

Magistay does not use tracking cookies. Our analytics provider (Plausible) is cookieless by design. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. Your rights

Since we do not store personal data beyond the active session, there is no persistent data to access, correct, or delete. For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@magistay.com.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Our approach to cookies

Magistay is designed to respect your privacy. We do not use tracking cookies.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service that does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track you across websites or devices. Plausible is fully GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant.

Third-party platforms

When you click through to a booking platform (Booking.com, Expedia, etc.), that platform may set its own cookies subject to its own cookie policy. We have no control over third-party cookies set after you leave our site.
